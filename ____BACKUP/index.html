About US

We’re headquartered in Mumbai, providing supply chain, logistics management and distribution channel, spread over the states of Bihar, Jharkhand, West Bengal, Maharashtra and Odisha and expanding rapidly nationally.

Ashoka Group Of Enterprises

The foundation of this group was laid in the late 1980’s by our founder Shri Ashok Kumar Poddar in Darbhanga Bihar.

With his vision, mission and hard work the group is now a leading name for supply chain and logistics with increasing presence nationally.

Group Statistics

  • The Group volumes are 8 Lakh MT Rake/Cargo Handling and Transportation of 15 Lakh MT of Cement on an average per annum.
  • The Group processes approx 1000 orders per day.
  • The Group caters to approx 1500dealers and retailers on a daily basis.
  • Group also expedites in handling and transportation of Clinker, Gypsum, and Aggregates.
  • The Group is involved in construction activity for warehouses, buildings and bridges.
  • The Group is coming up with its logistics park with E2E facility located at the INDIA-NEPAL trade corridor.
  • The Group is coming up with a Slag grinding unit in Surat (Guj) for production of GGBS.

FOUNDER

SHRI ASHOK KUMAR PODDAR

A professional graduate with 30 + years of experience in transportation, Clearing & forwarding activities, Ware house management and sales of Cement, Paint, TMT bars, Chemicals and other construction related products.

Our founder started this business as a small cement retailer, who used his two-wheeler to move in the lanes of Darbhanga with a single bag of cement in search of customers.

Later on having taken a hand loan from his father of Rs. 4lakhs at 15% interest in 1984-85 and starting a retail outlet in by the name of Ashoka Enterprises. Very soon the firm became a reputed name in the retail market for building materials.
